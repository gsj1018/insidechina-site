About InsideChina

The story, the mission, and the person behind the insights.

Hi, I'm gsj1018

I'm a former used car e-commerce entrepreneur who spent years building businesses across both Chinese and Western markets. After watching too many Western entrepreneurs stumble over cultural misunderstandings, misinformation, and missed opportunities in China, I decided to write about what I actually learned.

Why InsideChina?

Western media covers China in two modes: either fear-and-scare stories or surface-level "here's how to make money in China" fluff. What I needed — and couldn't find — was honest, practical, nuanced information from someone who'd actually done the work.

InsideChina is that resource. I write about China's digital economy with the clarity and candor I wish I'd had when I started. No agenda, no government angle, no cultural stereotypes — just what works and what doesn't.

What You Can Expect

  • Real experience — I've built and shipped products across both markets
  • Western perspective — I explain China to an audience that thinks differently, not to Chinese readers
  • Actionable insights — Every article should leave you with something you can actually use
  • Honest takes — I'll tell you what sucks about China's market, not just the upside

The Business Model

InsideChina is self-funded and growing. Revenue comes from affiliate commissions (I recommend tools and platforms I actually use), digital products (guides and reports), and eventually brand partnerships. The blog content is always free — my paid products are for people who want the deep-dive version.
