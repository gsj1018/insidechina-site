Building ChinaSource to help Western entrepreneurs source smarter

Why I built this site

I spent 3 years navigating China sourcing as a Westerner. I have ordered from Alibaba, 1688, AliExpress, and direct factory contacts. I have made every mistake there is: paid deposits to scammers, ordered products that arrived broken, miscalculated shipping costs, hired agents who ghosted me mid-order, and overpaid by 200% on my first bulk order because I did not know 1688 existed.

This site exists because none of the resources I needed existed in one place. I had to piece together knowledge from scattered Reddit threads, YouTube videos in Chinese, and expensive trial and error. ChinaSource is the resource I wish I had from day one.

My background

I have worked in used car e-commerce and sales, which taught me the fundamentals of cross-border trade, margin management, and customer trust. Those skills transferred directly to China sourcing -- the principles are the same even if the logistics are different.

I am based in China and have firsthand access to suppliers, factories, and markets that most Western buyers never see. I use this to test products, verify quality, and find the real price points -- not the inflated export prices.
