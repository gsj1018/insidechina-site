Top 20 Products to Dropship from China in 2026

Data-backed product picks with real margin calculations. These categories are winning right now on Shopify, Etsy, and Amazon.

How I Pick These Products

There are three filters I apply before recommending any product:

  1. Weight and shipping cost -- under 500g when possible, to keep shipping cheap
  2. Visual appeal -- will this look good in a product photo and unboxing video?
  3. Margin room -- can I sell this for 3-5x the sourcing cost?

The 20 Best Product Categories

1. LED Strip Lights

Sourcing cost: $1.50-$4.00 | Sell for: $15-$35
Huge on Amazon and Shopify. App-controllable RGB strips are hottest. Easy to install, great photos.

2. Wireless Earbuds (Budget Tier)

Sourcing cost: $3-$8 | Sell for: $25-$55
Replaceable. People lose one earbud and buy a whole new pair. Target budget-conscious buyers.

3. Phone Grip / PopSocket Clones

Sourcing cost: $0.30-$1.00 | Sell for: $8-$18
Ultra-light shipping, high perceived value, easy to customize with designs.

4. Portable Bluetooth Speakers

Sourcing cost: $5-$15 | Sell for: $35-$80
Durable, good margins, popular gift item. Look for water-resistant models.

5. Yoga Mats and Fitness Accessories

Sourcing cost: $3-$10 | Sell for: $25-$50
Fitness accessories keep trending. Non-slip mats, resistance bands, massage guns are hot.

6. Kitchen Gadgets

Sourcing cost: $2-$8 | Sell for: $20-$45
"As seen on TikTok" kitchen gadgets drive massive impulse buys. Garlic choppers, egg separators, silicone baking molds.

7. Pet Products

Sourcing cost: $3-$12 | Sell for: $25-$60
Pet owners spend freely. Elevated feeders, self-grooming brushes, cat tunnels, dog toys.

8. Phone Cases

Sourcing cost: $0.80-$3.00 | Sell for: $15-$30
High return rate on Amazon (not for FBA), but great for dropshipping. Update designs seasonally.

9. Travel Accessories

Sourcing cost: $2-$8 | Sell for: $20-$45
Neck pillows, packing cubes, universal adapters, eye masks. Great for TikTok travel content.

10. Wireless Phone Chargers

Sourcing cost: $3-$7 | Sell for: $25-$50
Standard now but Qi-certified models still sell well. Look for slim, fast-charging models.

How to Calculate Margins

The formula I use:

Selling Price - (Product Cost + Shipping + Platform Fee 10% + Payment Processing 3%) = Gross Profit

Gross Profit / Selling Price x 100 = Margin %

Example with LED strip lights:
Sell at $25. Product costs $3. Shipping averages $2.50. Platform fee = $2.50. Payment processing = $0.75.
Gross profit: $16.25. Margin: 65%. That is a good product.

Product Selection Mistakes

  1. Chasing trending products too late -- by the time a product is trending on TikTok, the margin window has closed
  2. Ignoring weight -- a $3 product that weighs 800g will cost $8+ to ship, killing your margin
  3. Not checking competitors -- if Amazon already sells the exact item for $10, you cannot win on price
  4. Picking fragile products -- glass, ceramics without padding, and electronics are the most returned. Returns eat profits.

My #1 Tip for Product Selection

Pick 3-5 categories from this list, order 3-5 samples from each on AliExpress, evaluate the quality yourself, then commit to 1-2 categories to go deep. Focus wins.
